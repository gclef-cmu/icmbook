# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-08-transpose
Load a short recorded sound and play it back at half and double speed. Describe
what happens to pitch, duration, and timbre.
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-08-table
Build a wavetable oscillator from a single stored cycle. Verify that doubling the
phase increment raises the pitch by an octave.
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-08-morph
Create two contrasting wavetables and morph between them over a few seconds.
Describe the timbral result.
```
