Subtractive synthesis needs a source with plenty of spectral content to carve
into. Two common choices:

- **Rich oscillators** — sawtooth and pulse waves contain many harmonics.
- **Noise** — *white noise* contains all frequencies at roughly equal power;
  *pink noise* falls off with frequency and sounds more natural.

Noise is the raw material for wind, breath, percussion, and countless texture and
effect sounds.
