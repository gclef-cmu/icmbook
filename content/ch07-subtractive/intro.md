# Subtractive Synthesis and Filters

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter07.svg
:width: 75%
:align: center
:alt: A bright spectrum being shaped by a lowpass filter.
``` -->

Where additive synthesis *builds* a spectrum, **subtractive synthesis** starts
with a harmonically rich source and *removes* parts of it with filters.

This chapter covers:

- **Noise and rich spectra** as raw material.
- **Filters**: lowpass, highpass, bandpass, and resonance.
