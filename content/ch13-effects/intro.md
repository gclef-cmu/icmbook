# Delays and Reverberation

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter13.svg
:width: 75%
:align: center
:alt: A sound and its echoes decaying over time.
``` -->

This chapter is about **time-based effects**: processes that combine a sound with
delayed copies of itself. The delay line is the single building block behind all
of them.

This chapter covers:

- **Delays**: echo, and the delay-based effects (chorus, flanger).
- **Reverberation**: simulating the sound of a space.
