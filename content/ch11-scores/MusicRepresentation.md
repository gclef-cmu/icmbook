A **score** is data that describes music. At minimum, each note carries a
*pitch*, a *start time*, a *duration*, and a *loudness*. Richer representations
add articulation, instrument assignment, and nested structure (phrases,
sections).

Time can be measured in *seconds* (absolute) or in *beats* (relative to a tempo);
beats make tempo changes easy.
