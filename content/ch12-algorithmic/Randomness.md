Random choice is a long-standing compositional tool. The art is in *shaping* the
randomness so the result is interesting rather than arbitrary.

- **Uniform choice** — every option equally likely; often too chaotic alone.
- **Weighted choice** — bias toward certain pitches or rhythms.
- **Constrained random walk** — each step is a small random change, producing
  smooth, melodic contours.
