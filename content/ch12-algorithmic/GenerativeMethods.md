Beyond raw chance, several **generative** techniques impose structure:

```{list-table}
:header-rows: 1

* - Method
  - Idea
* - Markov chains
  - Choose the next note from probabilities conditioned on recent notes.
* - Grammars / L-systems
  - Expand symbols by rewrite rules to grow musical structure.
* - Cellular automata
  - Evolve a grid of states; read music from the patterns.
* - Rule systems
  - Encode stylistic constraints and search for sequences that satisfy them.
```

Each gives the composer a different kind of control — statistical, hierarchical,
or constraint-based.
