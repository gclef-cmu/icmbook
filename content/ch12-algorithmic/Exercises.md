# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-12-walk
Generate a melody with a constrained random walk over a major scale. How do step
size and range affect the result?
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-12-markov
Build a first-order Markov chain from a melody you like, then generate a new
melody from it. How recognizable is the source style?
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-12-reflection
In a short paragraph, argue for or against the claim that an algorithmically
generated piece is "composed" by the programmer.
```
