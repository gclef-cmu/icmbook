# Algorithmic Composition

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter12.svg

If a score is data and a sequence is a program, then a *composition* can itself
be generated by an algorithm. This chapter introduces **algorithmic
composition**.

This chapter covers:

- **Randomness**: chance operations and controlled unpredictability.
- **Generative methods**: rules, grammars, and processes that compose.
