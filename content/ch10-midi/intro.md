Parts 1 and 2 were about *sound*. Part 3 shifts to *control*: describing what
should be played, and when. We begin with MIDI, the long-standing standard for
musical control data.

This chapter covers:

- **MIDI basics**: notes, velocity, channels, and messages.
- **Control signals**: continuous parameters and how they shape performance.
