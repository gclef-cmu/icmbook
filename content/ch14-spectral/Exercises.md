# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-14-spectrogram
Compute and display the spectrogram of a recorded sound. Identify a feature in
the image and explain what you hear at that moment.
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-14-framesize
Recompute the spectrogram with a much shorter and a much longer frame. Describe
the time–frequency resolution trade-off you observe.
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-14-stretch
Time-stretch a sound to twice its length without changing its pitch. What
artifacts, if any, do you hear?
```
