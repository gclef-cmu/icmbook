<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter14.svg

The final chapter moves into the **frequency domain**. Instead of manipulating
samples directly, we analyze a sound into its frequency content, transform that,
and resynthesize.

This chapter covers:

- The **short-time Fourier transform** (STFT) and the spectrogram.
- **Spectral processing**: time-stretching, pitch-shifting, and filtering.
