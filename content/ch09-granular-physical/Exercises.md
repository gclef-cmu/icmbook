# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-09-density
Build a granular texture from a single recorded sound. Vary the grain density
from sparse to dense and describe the perceptual change.
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-09-karplus
Implement the Karplus–Strong algorithm and pluck a string. How does the delay
length relate to the pitch?
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-09-compare
Synthesize a plucked-string sound twice: once with additive synthesis and once
with Karplus–Strong. Compare the effort and the realism of each.
```
