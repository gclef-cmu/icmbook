This chapter covers two further synthesis families: building sound from tiny
**grains**, and simulating the **physics** of real instruments.

This chapter covers:

- **Granular synthesis**: clouds and textures from short grains.
- **Physical modeling**: simulating strings, tubes, and membranes.
