# Computer Music Principles

Welcome to *Computer Music Principles*, the companion text and course site for
CMU's Introduction to Computer Music (15-322/622). It teaches the principles of computer
music theory and practice from a computer science perspective in Python.

- **Course Information** — resources and projects for students taking the course.
- **Textbook** — the principles of computer music, with runnable Python examples.

See [About this book](about.md) for details and authorship.
