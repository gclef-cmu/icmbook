# Oscillators and Additive Synthesis

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter04.svg

:alt: Several sine waves summing into a richer waveform.

The oscillator is the most basic sound generator. This chapter introduces
oscillators and shows how summing many of them — **additive synthesis** — builds
complex timbres.

This chapter covers:

- **Oscillators**: sine, and the classic geometric waveforms.
- **Additive synthesis**: building timbre from the harmonic series.
