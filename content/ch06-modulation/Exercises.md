# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-06-sidebands
A carrier at 600 Hz is amplitude-modulated by a 150 Hz modulator. At what
frequencies do the sidebands appear?
```

````{solution} ex-06-sidebands
:class: dropdown
At $f_c \pm f_m = 600 \pm 150$, i.e. 450 Hz and 750 Hz (plus the carrier at
600 Hz).
````
