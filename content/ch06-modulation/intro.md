# Modulation Synthesis

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter06.svg

**Modulation** means using one signal to control a parameter of another. It is a
remarkably efficient route to rich, evolving timbres.

This chapter covers:

- **Amplitude modulation (AM)** and ring modulation.
- **Frequency modulation (FM)** and the modulation index.
