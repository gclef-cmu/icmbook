# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-01-listening
In a few sentences, describe a piece of music or sound design that you think
*could not* have been made without a computer. What specifically makes it
computational?
```

```{exercise}
:label: ex-01-synth-vs-control
Classify each task as **sound synthesis** or **composition and control**:

1. Designing a bell-like timbre.
2. Deciding the rhythm of a drum pattern.
3. Adding reverb to a recording.
4. Generating a melody from a random process.
```

````{solution} ex-01-synth-vs-control
:class: dropdown
1. Synthesis — 2. Control — 3. Synthesis — 4. Control.

Tasks 2 and 4 decide *what* happens musically; tasks 1 and 3 shape the *sound*
itself. Many real projects combine both.
````
