This opening chapter sets the stage for the rest of the book. We will ask what
*computer music* actually means, look briefly at how the field developed, and
preview the tools and ideas the book is built on.

This chapter covers:

- What we mean by **computer music**, and the questions the field asks.
- A short **history** of the people, machines, and ideas that shaped it.
- An orientation to **Pyquist** and how the book is structured.
