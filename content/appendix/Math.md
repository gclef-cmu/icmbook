## Example: the sinusoid

A pure tone is described by a sinusoid

$$
x(t) = A \cdot \sin(2\pi f t + \phi),
$$

where $A$ is amplitude, $f$ is frequency in hertz, $t$ is time in seconds, and
$\phi$ is the initial phase. Most of the synthesis chapters build on this single
equation.
