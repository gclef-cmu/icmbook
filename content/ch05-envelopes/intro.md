# Envelopes and Amplitude

<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter05.svg
:width: 75%

:alt: An ADSR amplitude envelope shaping a tone.
``` -->

A steady tone sounds artificial because real sounds *change over time*. This
chapter is about **envelopes** — signals that shape a sound's parameters as it
plays.

This chapter covers:

- **Amplitude** and how loudness is controlled.
- The **ADSR** envelope and other shaping curves.
