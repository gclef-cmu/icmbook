# Exercises

```{exercise}
:label: ex-02-samplecount
A mono audio file is 3.5 seconds long at a sample rate of 48 kHz. How many
samples does it contain?
```

````{solution} ex-02-samplecount
:class: dropdown
$3.5 \times 48000 = 168000$ samples.
````
