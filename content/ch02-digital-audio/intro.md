<!-- ```{image} ../images/chapter02.svg

Before we can synthesize sound, we need to know how a computer stores it. This
chapter covers how a continuous pressure wave becomes a sequence of numbers.

This chapter covers:

- **Sound** as a physical wave and its perceptual attributes.
- **Sampling** — measuring a signal at regular time intervals.
- **Quantization** — representing each sample with finite precision.
